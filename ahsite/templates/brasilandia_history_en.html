The History and memory of Brasilândia/MS

This book covers the initial steps of the journey that the city of Brasilândia / MS undertook when it was not even aware of itself. The news printed in the local newspapers, the notes of the first travelers, historical documents, letters and poetry scribbled on school notebooks, photographs and the flashes impregnated in the memory of the old residents who built it with sweat on their faces and calluses in hands every inch of this floor. These are the characters that are scattered across the pages of this work. Since the first occupations and the clashes faced by the oldest explorers, even the pioneers of the development of the so-called civilizing process responsible for the emergence of the city, behold, on here they gather, at the limits of the City of Hope, without distinction.

And that was how the author managed to collect over twenty years of research, with obstinate patience, be by means of a declaration, whether by panning old newspapers and scattered notes kept in the chests of the memory, the content of the memory discussed here ... And he suggested to himself the possibility of building a for this place.

In this endeavor, dedicated and altruistic search, everything served as the basis for the construction of this work, always on perspective of valuing heroic deeds that, most of the time without a face and without a voice, They fought and won, broadening the horizon of Brasiland heraldry. It also does justice to those who have lost in those who fell, without, however, having finished giving their testimony and toil.

Two interests emerge when describing the trail of the pioneers of this city: the first, the concern and the second, a social concern, both of which look at the steps no people path of development and progress. It seeks, above all, to value the achievements of men and women who, with vigor and audacity, wrote the lines that sedimented the culture and history of those who lived and still live on this land. With this concern, the registration, name by name, of many of the characters that were part, in some way, of the structures and institutions of this city I saw along From years, where few were revered in life. In spite of the fact that both of them also deserve to have been remembered.

In other words, this work wants to give opportunity to those who, subject to their own history, literally now, they have the opportunity to take it back from the memory that, little by little, all days, they move away from us a little, as if the past and its youth were saying goodbye to their people. It gives pride to the citizens portrayed here and gives them awareness and courage for the feeling of belonging to the soil that welcomed them so well.
